What ties it together

Impact only counts when you can measure it.

And it only lasts when people feel ownership of it. I bring the strategy and the structure, but I also bring the human side — the listening, the culture work, and the relationships that turn a one-time initiative into something a community can rely on.

  • Strategy and structure. The measurement, partners, and reporting that make impact provable.
  • The human side. The listening and culture work that earns real ownership.
  • Built to last. Programs still running and students still served long after the ribbon is cut.
