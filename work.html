Annual
Amplitude

Launched and led Amplitude's annual DEI Report

I launched Amplitude's DEI Report in 2022 — its first edition covering the 2021 business year — and have led every edition since, most recently March 2026. Each one shares public, radically transparent data on the company's diversity goals and progress, grounding its commitment to an inclusive culture in numbers, not slogans. As I put it: "If we lead with inclusion, diversity will follow."
